Question No. Q00001Q - ID : Q0000236
A project has the following features:
 
- The execution phase has begun.
- Immediate adherence is required to a new law.
- The project's budget, timeline, and scope will be impacted by this law.
 
What are three possible actions to ensure compliance? (Choose three)
Choice 1
Start taking measures to avoid the risk.
Choice 2
Make sure to revise the project management plan.
Choice 3
Raise the concern to higher-level management.
Choice 4
Record the problem in the issue log.
Choice 5
Commence the process of requesting changes.
Selected Choice:
3,4,5
Process Group :
BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT
Task/K. Area :
Task 1 - Plan and manage project compliance
Justification:
"As compliance is compulsory and will affect the project's range, timetable, and expenses, a request for change must be made. Additional actions may include updating the issue log and raising the issue to higher management in order to locate a prompt solution that could eventually be resolved through a change. Since complying with the law is mandatory, avoiding the risk is not an option. At this point, updating the project management plan is not considered a corrective action.

Domain: Business Environment;
Task: Plan and manage project compliance;
Reference: PMBOK®GUIDE Seventh Edition Page #27, Section 3.1;"
