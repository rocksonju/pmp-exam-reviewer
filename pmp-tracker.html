The Four Pillars of the Exam

  • ✓
    Servant Leadership (People): You are a bulldozer clearing obstacles for the team. You do not micromanage or assign tasks in Agile. You facilitate.
  • ✓
    Assess Then Act (Process): You never fire someone, escalate immediately to the sponsor, or blindly implement a change without assessing the impact first.
  • ✓
    Strict Change Control (Process): In Predictive projects, ANY change to a baseline must go through the CCB. Period.
  • ✓
    Value Driven (Business): Projects exist to deliver business value. Regulatory compliance takes priority over everything.
